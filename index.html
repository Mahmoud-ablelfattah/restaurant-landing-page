Our Story

A Modern Culinary Experience

Founded in 2015, Savory Bites has redefined contemporary dining by blending traditional techniques with innovative flavors. Our chef, James Wilson, brings 20 years of international experience to create dishes that tell a story.

Our elegant yet comfortable space features an open kitchen where guests can watch our culinary team at work. We pride ourselves on sustainability, sourcing over 80% of our ingredients from local farms and producers.
